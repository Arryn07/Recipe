Peanut Butter Cookies

Description:

Peanut butter cookies are one of my all-time favorite desserts, and they are so simple and easy to make.

These cookies are perfect because they only require ingredients that you already have in your cabinets! This is one of the reasons why I make these cookies so often, as I don't have to go to the grocery store to pick up any special ingredients. I know that I have all that I need to construct an amazing dessert. Enough about my love for peanut butter cookies, already! Let's get into the recipe:

Ingredients:

  • Butter: 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted
  • Peanut Butter: 1 cup crunchy OR smooth
  • Sugar: 1 cup
  • Brown Sugar: 1 cup packed
  • Eggs: 2 large
  • All-Purpose Flower: 2 1/2 cups
  • Baking Soda: 1 1/2 teaspoons
  • Baking Powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon

Steps:

  1. Beat butter, peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth; beat in eggs.
  2. Stir flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt into a separate bowl; stir into butter mixture until dough is just combined. Chill cookie dough in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. Roll dough into 1-inch balls and place 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets. Flatten each ball with a fork, making a crisscross pattern.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden, about 10 minutes. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
