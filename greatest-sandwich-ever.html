Greatest Sandwich Ever

Description:

This sandwich would make any person--or dog--faint!

Are you ready to recieve instruction from the realm of creation above you for the sandwich you are about to conceive? If so, put on your favorite music, do a little dance, take a deep breath, and prepare yourself for the beauty to come.

Ingredients:

  • Baguette: 1 whole
  • Cream Cheese: 1 tablespoon
  • Pickles: preferably Prismo's pickles, but storebought is fine.
  • Fresh Dill: some
  • Boiled Eggs: 2 large
  • Bird: from the window
  • Common Cucumber 1 medium
  • Roma Tomato: 2
  • Sweet Yellow Onion: 1/2
  • Tears: for salt
  • Meat: 1 slab
  • Rosemary: 2 sprigs
  • Thyme: some
  • Bacon: 3 strips
  • Lobster Soul: 1 fresh

Steps:

  1. Wash vegetables
  2. Sharpen knife (exactly 6 strokes)
  3. Place rosemary and thyme in a vaccuum-sealed bag with the meat
  4. Cook the meat sous-vide at 135 degrees until cooked thoroughly
  5. Cut the baguette in half longways and toast it
  6. Spread the cream cheese onto each half of the toasted baguette
  7. Add the pickles from ya boy Prismo to one half
  8. Add the dill to the half without the pickles
  9. Dice and add the boiled eggs
  10. Add the bird from the window
  11. Slice and add the cucumber
  12. Slice and add the roma tomatos
  13. Slice and add the sweet yellow onion
  14. Add the tears produced from cutting the onions to the sandwich for salt (to taste)
  15. Add the sous-vide meat
  16. Fry and add the bacon
  17. Boil a pot of water and quickly add a lobster, collecting its soul as it tries to escape
  18. Step back and admire your creation
